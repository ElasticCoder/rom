5C3A: ERR-NR - One less than the error report code
Read by the routine at ED_ENTER, and updated by the routines at ERROR_2, ED_DOWN, ED_ERROR, ED_COPY, MAIN_EXEC, LINE_SCAN and INPUT. The IY register is initialised to 5C3A by the routine at NEW, and reinitialised by the routine at STACK_BC. This system variable is accessed by some routines via the IY register at IY+$00.
Input
Output
ERR_NR 5C3A DEFB $00
