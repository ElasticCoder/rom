5C61: WORKSP - Address of temporary work space
Initialised by the routine at NEW, read by the routines at BC_SPACES, SET_HL, MAIN_ADD, LINE_RUN, INPUT and IN_ASSIGN, and updated by the routines at POINTERS, RESERVE and SET_MIN.
Input
Output
WORKSP 5C61 DEFW $5CCE
