5C3D: ERR-SP - Address of item on machine stack to use as error return
Initialised by the routine at NEW, read by the routine at ERROR_2, and updated by the routines at EDITOR, ED_ENTER, ED_COPY, CLEAR, GO_SUB, RETURN and INPUT.
