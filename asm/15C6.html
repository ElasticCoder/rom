15C6: THE 'INITIAL STREAM DATA'
Used by the routines at NEW and CLOSE.
Initially there are seven streams - +FD to +03.
Input
Output
STRMDATA 15C6 DEFB $01,$00 Leads to channel 'K' (keyboard)
15C8 DEFB $06,$00 Leads to channel 'S' (screen)
15CA DEFB $0B,$00 Leads to channel 'R' (work space)
15CC DEFB $01,$00 Leads to channel 'K' (keyboard)
15CE DEFB $01,$00 Leads to channel 'K' (keyboard)
15D0 DEFB $06,$00 Leads to channel 'S' (screen)
15D2 DEFB $10,$00 Leads to channel 'P' (printer)
